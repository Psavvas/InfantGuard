InfantGuard

An innovative vehicle safety system for infants

InfantGuard is a comprehensive vehicle safety monitoring system designed to help prevent Pediatric Vehicular Heatstroke (PVH) incidents and injuries attributed to the Forgotten Baby Syndrome (FBS). InfantGuard can detect whether an infant is left unattended and automtically alert the caregiver via SMS text message. Furthermore InfantGuard monitors the vehicle's interior temperature and will automtically notify emergancy services with vehicle location, temperature and license plate, if temperatures reach dangerous levels.


Why InfantGuard

Every year in the United States, dozens of children die from heatstroke after being left unattended in vehicles. Since 1998, over 1000 casualties have occurred due to Forgotten Baby Syndrome (FBS). As its name implies, FBS is completely unintentional and accidental; the parent gets busy and forgets. Infants are at risk of fatal heatstroke at temperatures as low as 107¬∞F, and a vehicle's interior can reach 120¬∞F in just 30 minutes‚Äîeven on mild days. Immediate action is critical to prevent tragedy. InfantGuard aims to reduce these tragic incidents by providing caregivers with a reliable system to monitor their infants' safety in vehicles.
